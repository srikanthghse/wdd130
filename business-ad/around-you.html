Leucadia Donut Shoppe

Leucadia Donut Shoppe is a charming, family-owned bakery nestled in the heart of a coastal town. Specializing in handcrafted, gourmet donuts, our shop offers a delightful array of flavors and unique toppings to satisfy every sweet tooth. Our commitment to using high-quality ingredients ensures each donut is a delectable treat.

  • Wide Variety: Leucadia Donut Shoppe boasts an extensive menu featuring classic favorites, innovative creations, and even customizable options.
  • Local Flair: We source locally whenever possible, supporting nearby farmers and businesses to infuse our donuts with a touch of community flavor.
  • Events and Catering: Beyond daily offerings, we cater to events and celebrations, providing a sweet touch to weddings, parties, and corporate gatherings.
  • Donut Subscription: Indulge regularly with our convenient donut subscription service, delivering a fresh assortment of handpicked treats right to your doorstep.

Average Cost: The average cost for a single donut ranges from $2 to $3, with customizable and specialty options priced accordingly.

Anh Hong Restaurant

Anh Hong Restaurant is a beloved culinary gem specializing in authentic Vietnamese cuisine. Nestled in the heart of the city, our restaurant offers a diverse menu of traditional dishes crafted with the finest ingredients, delivering a true taste of Vietnam.

  • Authentic Vietnamese Cuisine: Anh Hong is renowned for its authentic pho, flavorful spring rolls, and a menu that captures the essence of Vietnamese gastronomy.
  • Cozy Ambiance: Enjoy a warm and inviting atmosphere, perfect for intimate dinners, family gatherings, or casual dining with friends.
  • Catering Services: Elevate your events with our catering services, bringing the rich flavors of Vietnamese cuisine to weddings, parties, and corporate functions.

A meal at Anh Hong Restaurant typically ranges from $15 to $25 per person.

Whitsunday Barber

Whitsunday Barber is your go-to destination for precision haircuts and grooming services. Located in the heart of the community, our experienced barbers provide top-notch services in a welcoming environment.

  • Expert Barbers: Our skilled barbers bring years of experience and expertise to ensure every haircut is tailored to perfection.
  • Personalized Styling: Whether you prefer a classic cut, modern style, or a beard trim, we prioritize personalized service to meet your unique grooming needs.
  • Appointment Convenience: Enjoy the convenience of scheduling appointments online or walk-ins, accommodating various preferences and busy schedules.

Haircut services at Whitsunday Barber typically range from $20 to $40, with additional charges for styling and beard services.
